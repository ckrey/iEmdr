iEmdr

Features

  • The iEmdr app supports psychotherapists in their Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing therapies.

  • The app allows to record sessions, link the sessions to clients from the address book, store the clients preferences and export the recorded session data via email.

  • The app runs on iPhone and iPad in all orientations, can be uses with Apple-TV and supports TV-screens or monitors connected via HDMI or VGA apators.

New features in Version 3.0

  • 5 move patterns including diagonal and figure 8.
  • 5 alternating sounds
